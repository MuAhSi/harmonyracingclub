About the Club

The Harmony Racing Club is a proposed student organization giving students hands-on experience in designing, building, and racing a budget-friendly endurance car in the 24 Hours of Lemons. We integrate STEM learning with teamwork, safety, and real-world problem-solving in an exciting motorsports environment.

Club Activities

  • Weekly strategic planning meetings
  • Hands-on car build and fabrication sessions
  • Comprehensive safety and compliance training
  • Active fundraising and sponsor outreach
  • Thrilling race day participation

Impact on Our School

  • Boosts reputation as an innovative STEM hub
  • Provides national exposure through Lemons events
  • Engages local businesses and community support
  • Promotes teamwork, leadership, and real-world skills

Roles and Structure

Mechanical Team

Engine rebuilding, brake systems, suspension tuning, and drivetrain optimization.

Electrical Systems

Wiring harnesses, lighting systems, data acquisition, and electronic troubleshooting.

Safety & Compliance

Roll cage construction, fire safety systems, driver harnesses, and race regulations.

Media & Marketing

Social media promotion, event documentation, sponsor relations, and brand building.

Logistics Management

Budget planning, project scheduling, parts procurement, and sponsor coordination.

Racing Driver

Selected through rigorous training programs and performance evaluations.
